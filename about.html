Founded in 1992 by Dennis Boerner, Strategic Software Solutions has been providing IT resources to companies in the Delaware Valley.

Starting with mainframe, cobol and foxpro, we have developed an extensive pipeline in the industry as technology has continued to grow at a rapid pace. We are a small, family company and we stay true to our core values- work smart, hard and with purpose. Following that simple rule and treating our clients and consultants with respect has allowed us to thrive in this increasingly competitive environment.

There are a lot of IT Recruiting, Consulting, Contracting, Headhunting and Permanent Placement firms around. We understand there are other options and that is why we strive to separate ourselves by being consistent. We have been able to succeed for twenty-seven years by earning the trust of our clients. We would welcome the opportunity to earn your trust.
